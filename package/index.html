About Us


The founding members of Precision Point Consulting FZC LLC are Mr. Joy K S & Mr. Sabu B M.

Mr. Joy K S comes into this organization with 15+ years of experience across various industries like automobile, consulting and meat industry where he has handled roles from sales, butchery, consulting & mentoring. He has been an exceptional performer in all the roles that he has handled.

In the meat industry, he started as a butcher and has more than 10 years of hands-on experience, including 2 years as a successful skilled butcher in Germany. During this period, he has guided and mentored over 100 professionals across various companies in Germany. He has also developed a course on modern butchery delivered via an online learning platform.

Mr. Sabu B M on the other hand is an Engineer by qualification and an entrepreneur by passion and experience. He has 20+ years of diverse experience in areas like Technical Project Management, Operations, Marketing, Public Relations, Resource Management (Exposure to Maximo for CMM & Asset Management), EHS, Recruitment, Business Development, IT, Admin Support, Documentation management & Office Administration.

They together have founded PPC FZC LLC, a UAE-based HR recruiting and consulting company with the intention to extend their services to the global customers. The Indian operations is backed with the MEA license. An MEA license refers to license issued by Govt. of India enabling compliant cross-border recruitment and placement of workers.
